The Best Laundry Practices for Summer Clothes

Summer clothes require special care to stay fresh and vibrant. Here are the best practices for keeping them in top condition.

How to Care for Your Summer Wardrobe

  • 1. Wash Clothes in Cold Water

    Cold water helps preserve the colors and fabric of lightweight summer clothes like cotton and linen.

  • 2. Air Dry Your Clothes

    Avoid using high heat in the dryer. Instead, let your summer clothes air dry to maintain their shape and fabric integrity.

  • 3. Use Mild Detergent

    Use a gentle, mild detergent to protect the fibers of your clothes and prevent fading.

  • 4. Store Clothes Properly

    Make sure your summer clothes are properly stored when not in use to avoid wrinkles, fading, and fabric damage.
